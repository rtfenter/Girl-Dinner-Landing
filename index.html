FAQ

Calm answers. Product honesty.

Is this a recipe app?

Not really. Girl Dinner Tonight is a dinner decision assistant. It gives you a primary suggestion and a couple of alternates — enough choice to feel agency, without overwhelming you.

Do you track anything about me?

No accounts. No streaks. No grading. The app is designed to avoid pressure loops by default.

How do saves and grocery lists work?

Saved meals live in the Saved tab. Grocery lists can only be created from saved meals — on purpose — so lists stay clean and relevant.

What does premium unlock?

Premium unlocks Gym Girly mode, 250+ additional meals, 8 additional meal packs, Weekly Meals, and unlimited saves and grocery lists — without adding notifications, streaks, or engagement traps.

Is this only for women?

It is designed with women’s lived experience in mind, but anyone who wants calm, realistic dinner decisions can use it.

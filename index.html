FAQ

Clear, calm answers. No hype.

Is this a recipe app?

No. Girl Dinner Tonight is a food decision assistant. It helps you choose a realistic option for tonight based on mood, time, method, and ingredients.

Do I need an account?

No. The app is designed to be low-friction and privacy-forward.

Is it only for women?

It is designed with women’s lived experience in mind, but anyone who wants fast, comforting, realistic meals can use it.

What about pricing or premium?

Premium is not finalized yet. The landing page keeps this intentionally non-specific.

FAQ

Calm answers. Product honesty.

Is this a recipe app?

Not really. Girl Dinner Tonight is a dinner decision assistant. It gives you a primary suggestion and gentle backups — enough choice to feel agency, without overwhelming you.

Do you track anything about me?

No accounts. No streaks. No grading. The app is designed to avoid pressure loops by default.

How do saves work?

Saved meals live in the Saved tab. They’re just for you — no scoring, no “streak,” no “do better.”

What does premium unlock?

Premium unlocks Gym Girly mode, 250+ additional meals, 8 additional meal packs, Weekly Meals, and unlimited saves and grocery lists — without adding notifications, streaks, or engagement traps.

Is this only for women?

It’s designed with women’s lived experience in mind, but anyone who wants calm, realistic dinner decisions can use it.
